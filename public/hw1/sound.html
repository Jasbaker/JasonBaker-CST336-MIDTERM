Sound Design



Sound engineers are technical professionals responsible for sound effects and sound positioning.
Key aspects involved in the sound engineering process for games can be composing, or creating the actual musical score for the game.
Another important part of many modern games is voice acting, and language localization, giving voices to the characters within the game in order to bring them to life.
