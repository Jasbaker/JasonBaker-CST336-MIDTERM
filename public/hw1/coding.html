In Engine Coding



Most of a games physics and graphics programming is all done in the game's respective engine.
Engines are sometimes built from the ground up in-house by development companies, and are then recycled for most of their projects.
At times engines can be licensed by third party game developers in order to simplify their process and development time.
Engines is the heart of what games game look and sound the way that they do.
