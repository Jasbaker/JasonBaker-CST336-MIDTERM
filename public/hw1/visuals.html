Visual Design



Artists involved in the production of a game usually begin by conceptualizing characters, enviorments and renders.
Eventually the development is oriented toward either 2D or 3D, and artists then may start to produce sprites, textures, meshes or user interfaces.
Artists involved in Game Development usually wear many hats, jumping between animating characters and actions, or making mock ups for potential designs.
